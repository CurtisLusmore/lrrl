This sequence goes by many names.

One such name is the Thue-Morse Sequence.

There are two items, left and right. Oh and one. Boop and beep.

It starts with a left. Or an oh, or a boop.

The sequence grows by repeating itself inverted.

Lefts become rights, ohs become ones, boops become beeps.
